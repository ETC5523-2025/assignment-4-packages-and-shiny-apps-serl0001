# haiexplorer

**haiexplorer** provides the Germany PPS 2011 HAI burden dataset and a Shiny app to
explore *DALYs = YLL + YLD*, cases and deaths across HAP, UTI, BSI, SSI and CDI.

- **Launch the app:** `haiexplorer::launch_app()`
- **Dataset:** ` hai_germany_2011` (with 95% intervals and YLL/YLD breakdown)
- **Article:** *Getting started* (see Articles tab)

## Why this app?
The interactive metric switch + YLL/YLD stacking helps users see whether burden is
driven by **mortality (YLL)** or **morbidity (YLD)**. Top-N and type filters guide a
clear conclusion.
